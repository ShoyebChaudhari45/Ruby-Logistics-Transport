About Ruby Logistics

Four Decades of Delivering Trust Across India

Ruby Logistics is a trusted name in integrated logistics and supply chain solutions, a proudly family-run enterprise built on ethics, reliability, and four decades of excellence.

1984
Founded by Ravinder Singh Tuteja, Ranjeet Pal Singh Tuteja & Gurvinder Singh Tuteja

Started operations in Aurangabad with a vision to build a reliable transport company in Marathwada.

2000s
Expansion & Corporate Partnerships

Now
8+ Corporate Clients, Pan India
